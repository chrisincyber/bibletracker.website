Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At BibleTracker, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

BibleTracker collects minimal data to provide you with the best experience:

  • Username: Your chosen display name within the app
  • Reading Progress: Chapters you've read and marked in your trackers
  • Memorization Data: Verses and prayers you've added to memorize
  • Settings & Preferences: Your theme choices, reminder settings, and Bible type
  • iCloud Data: When enabled, the above data syncs via your iCloud account

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Provide and improve the BibleTracker app functionality
  • Sync your data across your devices via iCloud
  • Send you reminders (only if you enable them)
  • Remember your preferences and settings

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in two places:

  • Locally: On your device for offline access
  • iCloud: In your private iCloud account (if sync is enabled)

We do not have access to your iCloud data. It's stored in your personal iCloud account, encrypted by Apple.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Period.

The only external services we use are:

  • Apple iCloud: For syncing (controlled by you)
  • Bible API: For retrieving Bible text (anonymous requests, no personal data)

5. Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics services. We do not track your behavior, reading habits, or app usage.

6. Children's Privacy

BibleTracker is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the app
  • Delete: Use "Reset All Progress" in Settings to delete all local data
  • Export: Your data is stored in iCloud and can be managed through your Apple account

8. Security

We take security seriously:

  • Data is stored securely on your device and in iCloud
  • We use Apple's security frameworks and encryption
  • We never transmit sensitive data to our servers

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app.

10. Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Contact us at yourbibletracker@gmail.com

Our Commitment

BibleTracker is built on trust and transparency. We will never compromise your privacy for profit. Your data is yours, and it always will be.
