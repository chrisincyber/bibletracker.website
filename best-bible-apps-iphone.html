Best Free Bible Apps for iPhone in 2025

Updated January 2025 | 8 min read

Looking for the perfect Bible app for your iPhone? With thousands of options in the App Store, finding the right one can be overwhelming. We've reviewed the top Bible apps to help you make the best choice for your spiritual journey.

What Makes a Great Bible App?

Before diving into our recommendations, let's consider what features matter most in a Bible app:

  • Multiple translations - Access to various Bible versions
  • Offline reading - Read without internet connection
  • Reading plans - Structured guidance through Scripture
  • Progress tracking - Monitor your reading journey
  • Memorization tools - Help with learning verses
  • No ads or paywalls - Distraction-free experience
  • iCloud sync - Keep progress across devices

Top Bible Apps for iPhone

2. YouVersion Bible App

One of the most popular Bible apps worldwide, YouVersion offers:

  • Thousands of Bible versions and languages
  • Reading plans from various ministries
  • Audio Bibles
  • Community features

Considerations: While free, includes promotional content and requires account creation for full features.

3. Olive Tree Bible App

Known for its study features:

  • Strong study Bible integration
  • Note-taking and highlighting
  • Cross-references

Considerations: Many premium features require purchase.

Why Choose a Dedicated Bible Tracker?

While general Bible apps are great for reading, dedicated tracking apps like YourBibleTracker offer advantages:

  • Accountability: See exactly where you are in your reading journey
  • Motivation: Streaks and progress visualization keep you engaged
  • Memory retention: Specialized tools help Scripture stick in your mind
  • Focus: No social features or notifications to distract you

Features to Look For in 2025

Bible app technology has evolved. Here's what modern apps should offer:

  • Privacy-first design: No tracking or data selling
  • Offline functionality: Full features without internet
  • Cross-device sync: Seamless experience across iPhone, iPad, and more
  • Customization: Themes, fonts, and reading preferences
  • Smart reminders: Gentle nudges to stay consistent
